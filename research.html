My current research is mainly focused on topological and geometric machine learning and its application in medicine. Previously I have worked on topics ranging from epidemiology, toxicology and geopyhsics to statistics and topological data analysis.

Projects

I am currently involved in the following funded research projects:

  • Retail Fresh
  • DISTINGUISH
  • Differentiable Multi-Parameter Representation of Complex Data
  • Exploring Multimodality in Weather and Climate Prediction

I have previously been involved in the following funded research projects:

  • Parameterized Complexity for Practical Computing
  • dCod 1.0 - Decoding the Systems Toxicology of Atlantic Cod
  • International epidemiology Databases to Evaluate AIDS Southern Africa collaboration (IeDEA-SA)
